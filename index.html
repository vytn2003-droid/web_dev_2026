Website Topic: Cybersecurity Basics

This website will share beginner-friendly cybersecurity concepts, common threats, and practical tips that help people stay safer online.

Common Threats

  • Phishing: Fake emails/texts that trick you into sharing passwords or payment info.
  • Malware: Harmful software that can steal data or damage devices.
  • Password attacks: Guessing, brute force, or credential stuffing using leaked passwords.
